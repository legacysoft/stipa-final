Programs

Our programs

  2. Programs

KUTUNZA PUNDA NI KUTUNZA JAMII PROJECT

Enhancing involvement of donkey owners in the general welfare of their donkeys within the five domains of nutrition, health, environment, behaviors, mental status and including their safety.

KUKU BORA PROJECT

Strengthening production of KALRO improved indigenous chicken among small holder farmers.

SORGHUM VALUE CHAIN PROJECT

Enhancing climate change adoption and market integrated small holder farming for certified Kamani and Seredo sorghum varieties for food, with high malting quality and yield among small holder farmers.

BEANS PROJECT

Increasing the adoption and production of KALRO improved bean varieties in order to improve food security, nutrition and income among small holder farmers.

SAUTI YANGU PROJECT

Increasing access to legal aid and assistance for Adolescent Girls and Boys, children in conflict with the law and persons with disabilities

MEN EMPOWERMENT PROJECT

Creating participatory environment where men and women; boys and girls work together as equal partners in actualizing peaceful co-existence and development at both family and community levels.

NATURE POSITIVE SOLUTIONS PROJECT

Scaling circular bioeconomic model specifically briquette making among women living in Manyatta urban settlements in Kisumu County, Kenya

COMMUNITY BASED HEALTH FINANCING PROJECT (CBHF)

Community Based Health Financing (CBHF) is a non-profit health insurance scheme targeting the informal sector, formed on the basis of an ethic of mutual aid and the collective pooling of health risks in which members participate in its management.

KISUMU DISTRICT SCHOOLS WATER AND SANITATION PROJECT

Improving access to safe water and sanitation for 6 primary schools in the target area to improve access, retention and performance by the children.

HIV | AIDS PROJECTS

Bringing to realization the human rights, sustainable development and literacy of HIV/AIDS/TB infected and affected community members through social and economic empowerment.
