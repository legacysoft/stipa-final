Board Officials

CHAIRPERSON SECRETARY TREASURER

GLADYS ZAWADI

Gladys Zawadi Karisa is a board member at STIPA. She’s currently working at KCB Bank Kenya with over 8 years of experience in the banking sector. Gladys has been able to achieve extraordinary business results during her career. She has continuously explored ways to improve the efficiency and effectiveness of service to enhance customer satisfaction and loyalty in her roles as a Customer Experience Consultant. She has previously worked as an online researcher at Adept Technologies

Gladys holds a Bachelor of Commerce in Finance from the Catholic University of Eastern Africa and a higher diploma in Purchasing and Supplies Management from the Kenya Institute of Management. She is currently pursuing her Masters of Science in Finance (MSc) at Kenyatta University.

Gladys joined the STIPA Board of Directors in 2023 and has contributed to the organization her insights with matters related to Finance.
